Our idea :

We want to provide individuals with a platform to join around several sports (running, cross-fit, strengthening, football, yoga...). Coaches can offer paid sessions to improve performance, but individuals can also meet for free to practice the sport they love.

How to choose?

The concept of the platform is quite simple. It works like a search engine with slots available. Verified professionals (coaches, sports supervisors) can offer slots around their specialties. Different ranges of levels are available depending on your goals. Then simply register for a session by registering on the platform. A secure payment module is available to pay for the session. Individuals can also offer sessions in order to simply share a moment of sport for free with other people. Sport is a major vector of sociability and helps maintain good health. It's the ideal tool to keep in shape while meeting new teammates.

Why call on us?

Our tool is a simple and effective way to find collaborative sports sessions. This also allows you to gain experience and skills in the sport practiced (breathing, posture, recovery, nutrition...). Our partnerships with several sports halls and private grounds allow you not to be bored to find the right place and the right time. Your future teammates are waiting for you so adopt the Together Sport solution as soon as possible!
